## How financial health is measured for Minnesota school districts

### 1. The district's own measures
- **Unassigned General Fund balance as a % of expenditures/budget.** School Board policy sets a **minimum of 5.0%**. At 6/30/2025 the district's unassigned balance was about **1.1%** of General Fund expenditures — below policy.
- **Priority-Based Budgeting (PBB).** The district's internal process for aligning spending to priorities and rebuilding fund balance; drives annual budget revisions.
- **Fund balance by classification** (nonspendable / restricted / committed / assigned / unassigned) tracked per GASB 54.

### 2. Minnesota Department of Education (MDE)
- **UFARS** (Uniform Financial Accounting and Reporting Standards) — the standardized chart of accounts every district must report on.
- **Statutory Operating Debt (SOD)** — Minn. Stat. 123B.81. A district is in "statutory operating debt" when its year-end net negative unreserved/undesignated General Fund balance exceeds **-2.5%** of operating expenditures. Districts in SOD must file a **special operating plan** (recovery plan) that MDE approves and monitors.
- **Fund-balance monitoring.** MDE tracks General Fund balance levels and trends across districts.
- **Reporting deadlines.** Audited data due to MDE by **Nov 30**; audited financial statement by **Dec 31**; SOD plans by **Jan 31**.

### 3. Office of the State Auditor (OSA)
- Requires the annual independent audit (GAAS, federal Single Audit Act, MN Legal Compliance Guide).
- Publishes **"Financial Trends of Minnesota School Districts,"** which benchmarks ratios such as the **quick/current ratio**, **short-term debt ratio**, and **fund-balance trends** across districts.

### 4. Third parties
- **Bond rating agencies (Moody's, S&P Global).** Assign credit ratings to the district's general-obligation bonds; ratings and the underlying analysis appear in bond Official Statements (available via MSRB **EMMA**, emma.msrb.org). This is the main external, independent read on financial health.
- **Independent auditor (LB Carlson, LLP).** Issues the annual opinion and any findings.
- **Municipal advisors (e.g., Ehlers)** and organizations like **MASBO** publish guidance/benchmarks (e.g., recommended fund-balance levels).
