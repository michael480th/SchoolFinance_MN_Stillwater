## MMB Payroll Data (State Employees Only)
*Source: https://mn.gov/mmb/transparency-mn/payrolldata.jsp*

**IMPORTANT**: MMB payroll data covers Executive and Judicial Branch state agencies only.
**School districts are NOT included.** ISD 834 staff would not appear here.
MDE (Minnesota Department of Education) state employees are included.
