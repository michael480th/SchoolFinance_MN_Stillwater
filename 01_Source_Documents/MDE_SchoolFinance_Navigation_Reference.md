## Levy Certification Process
URL: https://education.mn.gov/MDE/dse/schfin/Levy/

**Subpages:**
- Information for County Auditors: /MDE/dse/schfin/Levy/cty/
- Tax Shift Information: /MDE/dse/schfin/Levy/tax/

**Contact:** mde.levy@state.mn.us

**Key Process Dates:**
- Proposed levy certification to MDE and county auditor: September 30 (or next business day)
- Extension deadline (if granted): October 7
- Final levy adoption and certification: within 5 business days after December 20 (= December 28)
- Truth-in-Taxation Compliance Form to Dept. of Revenue: December 28

## Postsecondary Enrollment Options (PSEO)
URL: https://education.mn.gov/MDE/dse/schfin/pseo/
Contact: mde.pseofunding@state.mn.us

PSEO allows MN resident high school students (grades 10-12) to earn dual credit.
MDE pays tuition/required items directly to postsecondary institutions (PSIs).
Relevant to ISD 834 for calculating ADM adjustments (PSEO students reduce district ADM).
