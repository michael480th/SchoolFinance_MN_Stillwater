# OSA 2025 Legal Compliance Audit Guide — Summary of Changes
*Source: https://www.osa.state.mn.us/media/bncfiye0/2025-lcag-summary-web-prepped.pdf*
*Captured: 2026-07-01*

This document summarizes changes from the 2024 to the 2025 Legal Compliance Audit Guides.
Full guides at: https://www.osa.state.mn.us/audit-resources/audit-guidance/legal-compliance-audit-guide-sub-pages/2025/2025-minnesota-legal-compliance-audit-guides/

---

## School Districts (relevant changes)

On page 4-12 added a new item 9, citing Minn. Stat. § 123B.52, subd. 6a:

"If the school district disposed of school books, including library books, and textbooks
with accompanying materials without following Minn. Stat. §§ 471.345, 123B.52 or any
other provisions, did it do so by:
a. Donating them to a family of a student residing in the district, or
b. Donating them to a charitable organization under § 501(c)(3) of the I.R.S. Code?"

---

## Charter Schools (relevant changes)

- Revised reporting requirements to include: copy of any management agreement with a
  CMO or EMO; and copy of service agreement/contract totaling over 5% of audited
  expenditures (Minn. Stat. § 124E.16, subd. 1(c))
- Added new Part II citing Minn. Stat. § 124E.26 requiring charter schools to adopt a
  procurement policy covering:
  1. Conflict of interest provisions (§ 124E.14)
  2. Thresholds for purchases without board approval
  3. Competitive procurement thresholds:
     - $25,000–$175,000: two or more bids/proposals
     - Over $175,000: full competitive process (§ 124E.26, subd. 4a)
  4. Prohibition on splitting procurements to avoid thresholds

---

## Other Entities (non-school-district changes)

**Cities:** Changes to fire state aid distribution language; citation corrections.
**Counties:** Capital improvement bonds notice period changed from 14 days to 10 days
  (§ 373.40, subd. 2).
**Relief Associations:** Multiple citation and language corrections to pension/benefit
  payment sections (§§ 424A.05, 424A.092, 423A.022).
**Towns:** Fire state aid distribution language; citation for § 424A.08(d).

---

*OSA Legal Compliance Audit Guides are issued annually under Minn. Stat. § 6.65.*
*School districts are required to have legal compliance audits performed annually.*
