# OSA School Districts — Page Notes
*Source: https://www.auditor.state.mn.us/reports-data-analysis/local-government/school-districts/*
*Captured: 2026-07-01*

## Key Finding

The OSA school districts page lists reports issued **under OSA's own cover only**.
It does NOT include:
- Reports issued under the school district's own cover
- Audits performed by private CPA firms

**This means Stillwater ISD 834's standard annual ACFRs/CAFRs will NOT appear here.**
Those are already collected in `01_Source_Documents/ACFRs/` (5 years, FY2021–FY2025).

## What IS on the OSA school districts page

Reports listed are primarily:
- Investigative reports (fraud, misuse of public funds)
- Federal Assistance review reports (Federally Assisted Programs of Subrecipients)
- Review letters for districts with compliance issues

Total: 59 entries across 6 pages (as of July 2026).
