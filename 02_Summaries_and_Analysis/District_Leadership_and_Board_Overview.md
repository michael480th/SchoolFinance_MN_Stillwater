## Senior leadership (administration)

The superintendent's cabinet covers the standard functional areas of a district
this size: teaching & learning, finance, human resources, operations, technology,
student support, community education, and communications.

| Role | Person | Publicly documented background |

Previously superintendent of Albert Lea Area Schools; named **2022 Minnesota Superintendent of the Year** (MASA). During his Albert Lea tenure the district-wide graduation rate rose from about 68% (2012) to about 81% (2021). Holds a doctorate (Ed.D.). |

**Leadership continuity (factual).** From July 2020 through mid-2022, both the
superintendent and the district finance-director roles were filled on an interim
basis (Malinda Lansfeldt served as interim superintendent). Both roles have been
held by permanent appointees since 2022 — Funk (superintendent, July 2022) and
Schrul (finance, November 2022).

## Board of Education (2025)

Seven elected directors serve staggered four-year terms. Officers (chair, vice
chair, clerk, treasurer) are elected by the board annually.

\* Terms are approximate and should be verified against the official board page;
Minnesota school-board terms are four years and staggered. The November 2024
election seated Grcevich and Parker and re-elected Hockert, following the
departure of prior members Bev Petrie and Dr. Annie Porbeni.

**Board structure & practice (factual).** The board publishes meeting agendas and
packets through BoardBook; the district issues an annual audited Annual
Comprehensive Financial Report (ACFR) and operates under a board fund-balance
policy with a 5% minimum unassigned General Fund balance. Budget development runs
through the district's Priority-Based Budgeting process.
